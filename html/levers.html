Altura ONE / Levers Three levers, one ecosystem

Not three ideas. Three entry points into the same flywheel.

Each lever attacks a different barrier we measured in 281 surveys and 5 interviews, and each one pulls the customer one step deeper into Altura.

01 / Affordability perception

FreeStart BOGO

You subscribe to a broadband plan starting at ₹499, and you get six months of completely free mobile recharge (unlimited calls, messages, and 1.5GB daily data). Two services for the price of one. The consumer's first step into the Altura ecosystem costs them literally nothing extra.

79%would take this offer today
02 / Switching friction

AI Tool Ladder

Perplexity Pro and Adobe Express, bundled free from day one: fresh tools, no existing lock-in with any other provider. Then, at month three, ChatGPT Plus unlocks as a loyalty reward: the tool 82% of respondents already rely on, now free through Altura.

86.5%say this makes them stay longer
03 / Service fragmentation

IoT Ecosystem SIM

Buy a smart camera, speaker, or bulb from Croma, Reliance Digital, or Amazon, and find a free Altura SIM and a pre-configured hotspot inside the box. Altura becomes the invisible connectivity backbone of the smart home, and consolidating everything else starts to feel natural.

75.4%would move toward Altura for this
FreeStart gets them in AI Ladder keeps them IoT SIM pulls in the rest of the household
Why it holds together

85% want a customizable bundle or the option to choose.

Only 15% want a fixed, take-it-or-leave-it package. Consumers aren't rejecting consolidation. They're rejecting inflexibility. Each lever is a door in, not a cage.
